## Video Summary

In this episode of the Exit Five podcast, Dave Gerhardt interviews Amit Shaun, SVP of Marketing at Tatari, about how Connected TV (CTV) advertising is becoming accessible for B2B marketers. The conversation covers practical use cases for TV advertising, brand building, creative strategy, attribution, retargeting, AI-generated creative, and the relationship between brand and performance marketing.

## Research Notes

**Primary Theme:**
Brand marketing, Connected TV (CTV) advertising, and modern B2B demand generation.

**Content Style:**
Interview-style educational discussion with practical examples and industry insights.

**Target Audience:**
B2B SaaS marketers, CMOs, demand generation leaders, product marketers, founders, and growth teams.

**Why Selected:**
This interview explores an emerging marketing channel that many B2B SaaS companies overlook—Connected TV advertising. Dave Gerhardt guides the discussion toward practical applications, helping marketers understand when TV advertising makes sense, how it complements digital channels, and why brand-building remains essential alongside performance marketing. The episode also demonstrates Dave's interview style, which emphasizes practical education over promotion.

**Key Takeaways:**

- Connected TV is becoming accessible for B2B companies through lower-budget targeting and retargeting campaigns.
- Brand marketing and performance marketing should complement each other rather than compete.
- Customer testimonials and founder-led creative often outperform highly produced advertising.
- AI is reducing creative production costs, enabling faster experimentation with video campaigns.
- Successful B2B marketing combines education, credibility, and consistent brand visibility across multiple channels.
