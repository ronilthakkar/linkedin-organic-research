## Summary

In this training, Samantha McKenna explains the complete "Show Me You Know Me®" sales methodology, a personalization framework she developed after years of outperforming traditional outbound sales techniques. Rather than relying on mass email campaigns and generic outreach, she teaches sales professionals how to build authentic relationships through meaningful research, thoughtful personalization, and human-first communication.

The session covers prospect research, subject line strategy, email writing, follow-up sequences, LinkedIn outreach, discovery calls, and long-term relationship building. Throughout the course, Samantha argues that quality consistently outperforms quantity and that genuine personalization creates dramatically higher response rates than traditional sales automation.

## Key Takeaways

### Personalization creates a competitive advantage

Most sales teams rely on generic templates and automated outreach.

Samantha argues that authentic personalization remains rare, making it one of the easiest ways to differentiate from competitors.

---

### Research happens at three levels

Every prospect should be researched across three dimensions:

- The individual
- The company
- The industry or vertical

Understanding all three enables more relevant conversations and stronger relationships.

---

### Subject lines determine success

The subject line is the single most important part of an outbound email.

Rather than using generic phrases like "Quick Question," Samantha recommends highly specific subject lines that immediately demonstrate familiarity with the recipient.

---

### Avoid fake personalization

Personalization should never feel forced.

Simply mentioning a university, hometown, or hobby without connecting it to the conversation creates the opposite effect and reduces credibility.

---

### Value propositions solve business problems

Sales emails should focus on customer challenges rather than product features.

Instead of explaining what a product does, sellers should clearly communicate which business problems they help solve.

---

### Follow-up is part of the strategy

Effective sales outreach extends beyond a single email.

Samantha recommends thoughtful follow-up messages, LinkedIn connection requests, and relationship-building touches that continue the conversation without becoming intrusive.

---

### Discovery calls begin with curiosity

Rather than immediately pitching products, Samantha starts discovery calls by demonstrating preparation and encouraging prospects to discuss their business first.

This approach uncovers valuable information while building trust.

---

### Relationships outperform transactions

Throughout the training, Samantha emphasizes that successful sales careers are built on long-term relationships rather than short-term deals.

The goal is to become someone prospects genuinely enjoy hearing from.
