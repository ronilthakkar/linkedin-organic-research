# Ross Simmonds

Ross Simmonds is a B2B marketing strategist, entrepreneur, and the founder of Foundation Marketing. He is widely recognized for his expertise in content marketing, content distribution, SEO, AI visibility, and demand generation. Rather than focusing solely on content creation, Ross emphasizes that successful marketing comes from creating exceptional content **and** distributing it strategically across the channels where buyers already spend time.

## Why Ross was selected

Ross represents the modern evolution of content marketing. His work goes beyond blogs and SEO to explore how AI, YouTube, Reddit, review platforms, communities, and search engines collectively influence today's B2B buying journey.

His perspective complements experts like Amanda Natividad, Dave Gerhardt, and Chris Walker by focusing on **distribution as a competitive advantage**. Ross consistently argues that creating great content is no longer enough—marketers must ensure that content is discoverable, reusable, and visible across every platform buyers use to research solutions.

## Key Learning Themes

Across these resources Ross consistently emphasizes:

- Distribution is as important as content creation.
- AI increases the importance of strategy rather than replacing marketers.
- Buyers research products across many channels before visiting company websites.
- AI visibility requires brands to earn trust beyond owned media.
- YouTube is becoming a critical search and demand generation platform.
- Modern marketers should optimize for discoverability across search engines, LLMs, communities, and social platforms.
- Strong marketing combines great storytelling with intentional distribution.

## Why This Matters

For Product Marketing Managers and B2B marketers, Ross's work provides a modern framework for reaching buyers in an AI-first world. His insights help marketers understand how content, search, social media, YouTube, Reddit, review platforms, and AI assistants work together to influence purchasing decisions.

Rather than treating content marketing as publishing blog posts, Ross encourages marketers to think like media companies—creating valuable content once and distributing it strategically across every channel where their audience discovers, evaluates, and trusts information.

Together, the selected posts and interview provide both tactical guidance and strategic principles for building sustainable demand generation through content distribution, AI visibility, and modern B2B marketing.
