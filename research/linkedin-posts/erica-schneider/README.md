# Erica Schneider

## Why Erica Schneider?

Erica Schneider is a leading voice in B2B content strategy, positioning, and founder-led consulting businesses. As the co-founder of Duo Consulting and former Head of Content at Grizzle, she helps consultants, agency founders, and B2B service providers build premium businesses through clear positioning, repeatable systems, and educational content.

Her work focuses on turning expertise into scalable offers by combining strategic positioning, content marketing, and operational frameworks. Rather than chasing vanity metrics or rapid agency growth, Erica advocates for building profitable, high-margin micro-agencies that prioritize sustainability, client outcomes, and long-term freedom.

This research folder captures examples of Erica's thinking on positioning, content strategy, authority building, consulting frameworks, and modern LinkedIn organic marketing.
