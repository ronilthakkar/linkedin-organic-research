## Research Notes

**Primary Theme:**
Client success, positioning, premium consulting, and authority building.

**Content Style:**
Storytelling supported by social proof and outcome-focused examples.

**Target Audience:**
Consultants, agency founders, B2B marketers, fractional executives, creators, and service business owners.

**Why Selected:**
Rather than promoting her services directly, Erica Schneider demonstrates the value of her work by highlighting tangible client outcomes. The post relies almost entirely on social proof, making the audience infer the effectiveness of her coaching and positioning. It is an excellent example of authority-based marketing on LinkedIn, where customer success stories become the primary marketing asset instead of direct promotion.

**Key LinkedIn Techniques:**

- Opens with a humorous hook that immediately creates curiosity.
- Uses a long list of specific client wins to build credibility through social proof.
- Quantifies results with concrete revenue figures and business outcomes.
- Lets readers draw their own conclusions instead of making promotional claims.
- Ends with gratitude and humility, reinforcing authenticity while strengthening personal brand.
