## Full Post

We've built the best new event for B2B marketing professionals. Here are 26 reasons why you should come to Drive 2026!

1. Proof. The last two years the NPS (feedback after the event) has been in the 80s. Apple's NPS score is in the 60s. Are we a better company than Apple? Do we make more innovative products than the iPhone and the MacBook? Probably. OK maybe not. But it's great marketing isn't it??

2. Peer connection. Now more than ever you need to talk to REAL HUMANS about your marketing, not just Claude and Gemini and ChatGPT. Come get ideas and feedback you can put into play immediately at work.

3. Get caught up on what's actually working in B2B marketing right now, not what the hype train is saying on LinkedIn. This event will cure your FOMO.

4. Take time away from the office for yourself while still "working"—see what a professionally run event in your area of expertise feels like.

5. We're good at this. We pick the right venue, the right speakers, the food, music, vibes, etc. I guess they would call this taste.

6. Ben & Jerry's (the factory is 10 min down the road and we're bringing in fresh and not melted).

7. Seth Godin! The most timeless of marketing lessons.

8. Shake off the post-summer blues and get inspired to get back into work for the fall.

9. Travel experiences—if you've never been to Vermont, this is a great reason to come to Drive. You get to see a new place and you can also make a trip out of it and see more of the Northeast after too.

10. All of our workshop speakers are real B2B marketers actually doing B2B marketing and their workshops are all about what's been working for them.

11. Stowe, VT. Google "The Lodge at Spruce Peak."

12. September is the best month of the year in Vermont (my father-in-law is never wrong and has been saying this for 70 years).

13. The Exit Five events team does not miss a detail; we'll have shuttles from the airport so you can make a few new besties before you even get on site.

14. Everyone is staying in the same area either on-site or close by so you can hang out as long as you want.

15. S'mores.

16. 37% of attendees this year are return attendees. People keep coming back.

17. You'll sit next to someone at lunch and find out they solved the exact budget conversation you've been losing for six months.

18. If they let me DJ again...

19. You will meet people who live in the same city as you so you can keep hanging out after Drive (you wouldn't know this unless you go to Drive) and make new work friends.

20. We don't just sit in a conference room—there are hikes, workouts, and yoga.

21. THE TALENT SHOW. New this year. So many of you are singers, artists, comedians, even magicians—come share your talents and laughs.

22. We don't miss a detail. Anna and Allison physically timed how long it took to walk to places around the venue so they could make sure you have time to talk to your friends in between sessions.
