# Dave Gerhardt

## Why Dave Gerhardt?

Dave Gerhardt is one of the most influential voices in modern B2B SaaS marketing, particularly in founder-led marketing, community building, brand marketing, and content strategy. As the founder of Exit Five and former VP of Marketing at Drift and Privy, he shares practical insights drawn from operating high-growth B2B SaaS companies rather than abstract marketing theory.

Unlike marketers who focus primarily on tactical growth hacks, Dave emphasizes building memorable brands, creating authentic communities, and producing content that earns long-term trust. His work demonstrates how founder personality, creativity, and customer relationships can become durable competitive advantages.

This research folder captures examples of Dave's approach to content marketing, community-led growth, creative campaign execution, and educational marketing.
