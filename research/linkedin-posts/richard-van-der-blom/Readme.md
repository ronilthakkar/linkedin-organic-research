# Richard van der Blom

Richard van der Blom is one of the world's leading LinkedIn researchers and algorithm analysts. Unlike creators who rely primarily on personal experience, Richard combines large-scale platform research with practical consulting, publishing annual LinkedIn Algorithm Reports based on millions of data points.

## Why Richard was selected

Richard provides a data-driven perspective on B2B marketing and personal branding through LinkedIn. His research explains how the platform's algorithm works, how professionals can build authority, and how businesses can turn LinkedIn into a predictable demand generation channel.

His work complements practitioners like Amanda Natividad and Dave Gerhardt by focusing less on messaging and more on the mechanics of distribution, reach, engagement, and conversion.

## Key Learning Themes

Across these resources Richard consistently emphasizes:

- Authority is earned before LinkedIn amplifies it.
- Personal profiles outperform company pages.
- Valuable educational content compounds over time.
- Silent engagement matters more than public engagement.
- LinkedIn should be treated as a business platform, not a social media platform.
- Distribution and engagement are as important as content creation.
- Sustainable systems outperform short-term algorithm hacks.
