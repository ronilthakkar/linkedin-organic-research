## Full Post

A founder hired a salesperson after closing 3 deals.

It cost him 6 months and $80K.

The hire walked in with nothing to run.

The founder had closed his 3 deals on referrals and instinct. No documented process. No repeatable demo. No objection list.

So the new hire spent 3 months guessing.

Quota missed.

Both sides frustrated.

The founder blamed the hire and let him go.

Six months later he was back at square one, $80K lighter, with a sales process that still didn't exist.

Here's what I told him.

Your first 10 deals are research.

Only the founder can do that research.

Those deals teach you:

- The real objections.
- The actual buying triggers.
- The language buyers use when spending their own budget.

Close the first 10 deals yourself.

Document what worked.

Then hire someone to execute the playbook you've already proven.

Where are you on that count right now?
