## Full Post

I spent 90 days launching a B2B GTM.

I got 0 customers.

The fix took 6 weeks.

Here's what happened.

I was the marketer at a B2B startup.

My job was to launch go-to-market from scratch.

So I did what most marketers do.

I wrote the positioning.

I polished the website copy.

I built three pricing tiers.

Ninety days of work.

Zero customers.

I had built every piece of GTM without talking to a single buyer.

Every decision was a guess dressed up as strategy.

Then I rewound.

- Picked one narrow segment.
- Booked 20 buyer conversations.
- Wrote down their exact pain words.
- Replaced the website copy with their language.

The first customer came six weeks later.

Same product.

Same market.

Different starting point.

ICP comes first.

Always.

Product, messaging, and pricing all sit on top of it.

Skip it and you save zero time.

You only delay finding out you're selling to the wrong person.

Follow me (Shamal Badhe) for one GTM lesson like this every week.

All from real founder work.
