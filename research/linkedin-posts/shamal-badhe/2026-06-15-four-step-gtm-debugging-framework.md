## Full Post

Every broken GTM I've fixed needed the same four steps.

In the same order.

A founder I worked with had two customers after six months of selling.

We changed nothing about his product.

We changed the order he worked in.

### The sequence:

### 1. ICP

Pick one narrow segment.

Talk to 15 buyers.

Stop when five of them describe the same pain using the same words.

### 2. Messaging

Take their exact phrases and use them in:

- Your homepage headline
- Demo script
- Cold emails

Don't rewrite them.

### 3. Positioning

Identify the competitor you're most frequently compared with.

Explain your difference in one sentence using the buyer's language.

### 4. Pricing

Publish pricing based on the value buyers described during customer interviews.

Review and adjust pricing quarterly based on your win rate.

Sixty days after changing the sequence...

The founder had 11 customers.

Same product.

Same market.

Different order of execution.
