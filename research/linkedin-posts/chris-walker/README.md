# Chris Walker

## Why Chris Walker?

Chris Walker is one of the most influential voices in modern B2B SaaS go-to-market strategy, demand generation, and founder-led marketing. As the founder of Refine Labs and ENCODED, he is known for challenging conventional GTM frameworks and advocating for sustainable growth, customer-centric marketing, and first-principles thinking.

His content combines practical business strategy with bold opinions on marketing, sales, venture capital, startup growth, and organizational design. Rather than following established "best practices," Chris encourages founders and marketing leaders to rethink traditional assumptions and build systems aligned with today's business environment.

This research folder captures examples of Chris Walker's approach to GTM strategy, founder-led content, business philosophy, and modern B2B marketing.
