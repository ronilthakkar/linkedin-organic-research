## Why I Selected Chris Walker

Chris Walker is one of the most influential B2B SaaS marketing practitioners and the founder of Refine Labs. His content focuses on demand generation, founder-led marketing, GTM strategy, and challenging outdated B2B marketing practices with practical frameworks and real operating experience.

I selected him because his LinkedIn content consistently demonstrates how to educate an audience through storytelling, contrarian viewpoints, analogies, and actionable frameworks rather than promotional messaging. His posts are highly relevant to researching LinkedIn organic content strategy for B2B SaaS companies.
