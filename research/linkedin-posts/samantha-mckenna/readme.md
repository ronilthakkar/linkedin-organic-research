# Samantha McKenna

Samantha McKenna is the founder of #samsales Consulting and one of the leading voices in modern B2B sales, social selling, executive branding, and LinkedIn strategy. A former top-performing enterprise sales leader at LinkedIn, she is best known for developing the **Show Me You Know Me®** methodology—a relationship-first approach to outbound sales built on meaningful personalization rather than automation and volume.

## Why Samantha was selected

Samantha brings the sales perspective to this research collection. While many experts focus on marketing, content, or product positioning, Samantha specializes in how trust is built through authentic communication during the sales process.

Her work demonstrates that effective outbound sales is less about scripts and sequences and more about understanding buyers as people. She consistently advocates for thoughtful research, emotional intelligence, executive branding, and relationship-driven prospecting.

## Key Learning Themes

Across these resources Samantha consistently emphasizes:

- Buyers respond to people, not templates.
- Personalization should demonstrate genuine understanding rather than superficial research.
- Relationships outperform transactional selling.
- Executive branding strengthens both sales and hiring outcomes.
- Warm introductions are significantly more effective than cold outreach.
- Quality consistently outperforms quantity in outbound sales.
- Sales should create trust before attempting to create urgency.

## Why This Matters

For Product Marketing Managers, GTM teams, and B2B sales professionals, Samantha's work demonstrates how modern buyers expect personalized, respectful, and value-driven communication throughout the buying journey.

Her methodologies help organizations improve outbound performance while strengthening long-term customer relationships. Rather than relying on automation alone, Samantha advocates combining thoughtful research, empathy, and strategic communication to earn attention and trust.

Together, the selected posts and training provide both tactical techniques and foundational principles for building a modern sales process centered on authentic human relationships.
