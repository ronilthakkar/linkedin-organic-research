## Full Post

Agencies are on the spot right now.

Demand is at record highs, but the bar for keeping a client has never been higher.

People want execution, not a 90-page audit telling them to fix their broken pages. So... we changed how we work.

### The old way

A new engagement starts. Kickoff, then a month of auditing. Content creation kicks in a week or two after that.

Nearly six weeks before the client can show their leadership anything real.

### The new way

We still do the audit (if that's what the client needs). But we start content creation in week one while the audit runs in the background.

If the client has an established content inventory, we pick a few pages with potential and start updating them. We're looking for small, concrete wins that can compound.

We do that for two reasons:

1. Reduce time-to-value.
2. Build momentum.

Quick wins buy you momentum.

→ Momentum buys you trust.

→ Trust buys you the runway to do the deeper work that actually moves the business.

The alternative is showing up on day 30 with a Google Doc full of recommendations nobody is going to implement.

By then, the CMO is already looking for alternatives.

That's why we moved to this model at Minuttia - AEO Agency.

The market doesn't wait anymore, and honestly, it shouldn't have to!
