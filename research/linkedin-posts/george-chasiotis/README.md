# George Chasiotis

## Why George Chasiotis?

George Chasiotis is a B2B SaaS marketing strategist specializing in content marketing, AI Search Optimization (AEO), demand generation, and go-to-market strategy. As the co-founder of Minuttia and Restartt, he helps SaaS companies build scalable growth systems by combining content, SEO, AI search, and founder-led marketing.

His work bridges traditional content marketing with emerging AI-driven discovery channels. Rather than chasing short-term tactics or algorithm changes, George emphasizes first-principles thinking, customer understanding, and operational excellence. His content frequently combines original research, real client experiences, and practical frameworks that help B2B companies adapt to a rapidly changing search landscape.

This research folder captures examples of George's thinking on AI Search, content strategy, agency operations, demand generation, and modern B2B SaaS marketing.
