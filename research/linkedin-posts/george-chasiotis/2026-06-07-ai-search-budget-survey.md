## Full Post

We surveyed 599 marketers about AI search. I want to share two numbers from the budget section with you:

### 1. 39.9% of respondents allocate 0% of their SEO budget to AI search.

Easy to read that as "nobody's spending on this yet," but that's the wrong read.

**No dedicated budget ≠ No budget**

For a lot of these companies, the investments are already happening. They're just buried in the SEO line item and not labeled separately yet.

The money is moving.

### 2. Over half (50.2%) plan to increase their AEO budget by more than 10% in the next 12 months.

15.5% of them by more than 25%.

Forget whether a company has a dedicated line item today. What matters is whether they're reallocating toward this channel for tomorrow.

It seems that most of them are.

The appetite is already there.

Companies feel the need to invest, whether or not they have a dedicated budget.

So if you're sitting this out and have no plans to change that, the rest of the market is quietly deciding AI search matters.

You're the one who'll have to explain why you waited.
