# LinkedIn Organic Content Research for B2B SaaS

A curated research repository exploring how leading B2B SaaS practitioners build authority, educate audiences, and drive demand through LinkedIn organic content, product marketing, go-to-market strategy, executive branding, AI search, and founder-led growth.

The repository organizes public content from experienced practitioners into structured research that can serve as the foundation for future playbooks, competitive analysis, and marketing strategy.

---

# Project Objectives

This research project was created to:

- Identify high-signal B2B SaaS practitioners with consistent, practical expertise.
- Collect and organize recent LinkedIn posts and long-form video content.
- Document recurring messaging frameworks, positioning strategies, and content patterns.
- Build a reusable research repository for future product marketing and GTM analysis.

---

# Research Summary

The repository currently includes:

# Research Workflow

The repository follows a structured research process:

1. Select experienced B2B SaaS practitioners with consistent public content.
2. Review recent LinkedIn posts relevant to the research topic.
3. Collect long-form YouTube or podcast discussions where available.
4. Organize findings into structured Markdown documents.
5. Identify recurring frameworks and strategic themes across experts.

# Key Research Themes

Across the collected research, several recurring patterns emerge:

- Build authority before asking for conversions.
- Develop a clear positioning and point of view.
- Publish consistently around a focused area of expertise.
- Prioritize audience trust over algorithm optimization.
- Create original insights instead of repeating industry trends.
- Repurpose long-form ideas into multiple content formats.
- Treat LinkedIn as a long-term brand-building channel rather than a short-term lead generation platform.

---

# Repository Purpose

This repository is intended to serve as a structured knowledge base rather than a complete archive of every piece of content published by each expert.

The emphasis is on collecting high-quality, representative examples that demonstrate practical marketing frameworks and repeatable strategic thinking.

---

# Automation

The repository includes a small Python utility (`scripts/fetch_youtube_transcripts.py`) that can be used to download YouTube transcripts from the sources listed in `expert_video_sources.csv`.

Research organization, expert selection, and synthesis were completed through manual review of publicly available content.
